Welcome to the "Auriel's Reverie" website!

This site is ment to introduce the game concept of Auriel's Reverie.

In this 2D action-adventure platformer, you play as Auriel, an artist who has been going through a bit of an art block recently. After an exhausting night still unable to successfully complete a painting, Auriel ends up passing out at her desk only to find herself stuck in a dream world. This dream world seems to be made of Auriel's art pieces and they don't seem to be very happy with her!

Embark on a journey to traverse Aruiel's mind as she tries to escape her past creations, all while rediscovering why she became an artist in the first place!
